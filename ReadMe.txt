Digital Sound Keeper v1.0 [24.12.2014]
http://vegalogic.com/go/soundkeeper/

Prevents SPDIF/HDMI digital playback devices from falling asleep. Uses WASAPI, requires Windows 7+.
To close the program you may mute the Sound Keeper in the Volume Mixer or simply kill soundkeeper.exe process.
For autorun simply copy soundkeeper.exe to startup directory in the your Start Menu.

Features:
- Sound Keeper works in fully automatical mode and doesn't require any user interaction.
- Sound Keeper can keep sound on many digital sound outputs simultaneously (e.g. SPDIF and HDMI).
- Sound Keeper detects new digital sound outputs on the fly (e.g. when you are connected TV via HDMI).
